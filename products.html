Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my financial data?

We implement bank-level 256-bit encryption for all your data. Your financial information is never stored on our servers in plain text, and we conduct regular security audits to ensure your data remains protected.

Can I connect multiple bank accounts?

Yes! You can connect an unlimited number of accounts with our Premium and Business plans. The Free plan allows you to connect up to 2 accounts.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, we offer mobile apps for both iOS and Android platforms. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play Store to manage your finances on the go.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your premium features until the end of your billing period.
